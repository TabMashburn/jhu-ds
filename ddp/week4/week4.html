Interactivity

Points can be excluded from the computation that generates the linear regression line by toggling their include state or by zooming into a region to exclude all points outside the region.

The linear model automatically updates to reflect the current inclusion state of all data points.

Interactivity (cont.)

  • Single points can be excluded/included by clicking on the individual points
  • Groups of points can be excluded/included by selecting them and then clicking the “Toggle selected points” button
  • By zooming into the region of interest, all points outside the region can be excluded
  • To toggle the state of a point that is very close to another point, first zoom in, then change the state of the point, then zoom out
